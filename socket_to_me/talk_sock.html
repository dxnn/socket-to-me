Okay, but why Socket.IO?

  • Fallback for ignorant browsers
  • No native WS support in Node.js
  • Node to Node
  • Easy broadcast
  • Volatile messages
  • Disconnect / reconnect
  • Heartbeats and timeouts
  • Callback acknowledgements
  • Namespacing for multiplexing

Server support

  • pusher -- hosted service
